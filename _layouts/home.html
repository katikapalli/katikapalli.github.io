About

I'm a Predoctoral Researcher in the Vision Language and Learning Group led by Dr. Anand Mishra at IIT Jodhpur. My research focuses on multimodal deep learning, particularly vision-language models and data-centric approaches for low-resource and culturally significant domains. My current work includes developing dialogue-driven diagnostic models in collaboration with AIIMS and applying computer vision techniques to restore degraded ancient Indian manuscripts at IGNCA.
